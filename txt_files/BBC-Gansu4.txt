Diners sprint from cafe as deadly China earthquake hits
BBC News
19 December 2023
China's deadliest earthquake in 13 years struck Jishishan county at 23:59 local time (1559 GMT) on Monday.
The quake had a depth of 10km (6.2 miles), according to China Earthquake Networks Center (CENC).
Videos have been shared on social media of shops, restaurants and car parks shaking overnight.
At least 127 people have been killed and more than 700 reported injured.
